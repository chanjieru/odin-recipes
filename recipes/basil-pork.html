Basil Pork(Pad Kaprao)

Pad Kaprow (ผัดกะเพราหมู) is probably one of the most popular dishes in Thailand. It's a spicy stir fried dish made with chopped pork, basil, chilies, and garlic, and it's perfect for a one-dish meal over rice or as a side dish at a family style dinner. It's actually quite easy to make, and now you can try it at home with this restaurant perfected recipe from Worawut.

Ingredients

  • Ground Pork Loin
  • Holy Basil
  • Yardlong Beans
  • Thai Hot Chilies
  • Garlic
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Light Soy Sauce
  • Dark Sweet Soy Sauce
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Fry one egg and have it ready.
  2. Crush the garlic and chilies together, then fry them in oil for about 30 seconds.
  3. Add the ground pork and stir into the chili and garlic. Fry until well browned.
  4. Add the cut green beans, oyster sauce, sugar, and soy sauces. Fry until the pan starts to get dry, then add a small amount of water.
  5. Turn off the heat, then add fresh basil. Stir in well and serve immediately with steamed rice.
